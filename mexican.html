Tacos :

Preparation :

1. Prepare the Meat Filling Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onion and saut√© until translucent (about 2 minutes). Stir in minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the ground meat and cook until browned, breaking it into smaller pieces with a spatula. Sprinkle in chili powder, paprika, cumin, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Stir in the tomato sauce or salsa and simmer for 5 minutes until the mixture is well-coated and slightly thickened. Remove from heat and set aside.

2. Prepare the Tortillas Heat a dry skillet over medium heat. Warm each tortilla for about 30 seconds per side, until soft and pliable. Optional: For crispy taco shells, fold tortillas in half and fry in hot oil until golden..

3. Assemble the Tacos Lay a warm tortilla flat on a plate. Add a generous spoonful of the meat filling to the center. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheese. Add a dollop of sour cream and guacamole. Sprinkle with chopped cilantro and add jalape√±os for extra spice.

4. Serve Serve immediately with lime wedges for squeezing over the tacos. Pair with Mexican rice, refried beans, or tortilla chips for a complete meal.

5.Customizable Fillings: Swap the meat for grilled shrimp, fish, or saut√©ed vegetables for different variations. Crispy vs. Soft: Decide if you prefer soft tortillas or crunchy taco shells. Both work wonderfully!

Make Ahead: You can prepare the meat filling ahead of time and reheat it when needed.
